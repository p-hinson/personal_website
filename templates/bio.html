About Me

Who Am I?

Hello! I'm Patrick Hinson, a Data Scientist with a strong background in machine learning, statistical modeling, and large-scale data analysis. After double majoring in Statistics with a concentration in Econometrics and Computer Science from the University of Virginia in 2021, I began my career as a Data Scientist at the MITRE Corporation. I went on to get my M.S. in Data Science from Johns Hopkins University while working at MITRE and transitioning to working on my.uscis.gov. Outside of work, I enjoy rock climbing, weightlifting, and exploring, whether it be wandering around my home city of Washington, D.C., traveling abroad (I love mountains, and the D.C. Metro area does not have anything larger than big hills!), or surfing the internet. I am particularly interested in the spaces of biology, artificial intelligence, and their intersection! I try to stay up to date with traditional sports and esports alike. Along with sports, I love to think about the underlying systems that influence the things that I encounter day and how they can be modeled.
